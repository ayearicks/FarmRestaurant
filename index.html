Gourmet Farm to Table Dinners

For the freshest fine dining experience in our area, reserve your place at one of our renowned farm to table dinners. Our one of a kind dinners utilize the freshest ingredients available, with no additives necessary. As a dinner guest, you'll get to experience a night of culinary excellence from our team of top ranked chefs in the area, as they serve you a four course meal with locally sourced fruits, vegetables, and meats. Without the need for expensive shipping costs and bulk freezers, everything is harvested right from this very farm daily.

Enjoy the finest dining available in East Washington State, and book your fresh dinner at Farm to Table today. You will fall in love with the atmosphere, the music, and the exquisite food.
